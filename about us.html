Team Delta Cosmos deals with the primary objective of "Space Selfie" which is basically taking pictures from space. We plan to obtain this goal by launching a 3U cubesat to the Low Earth Orbit region(Polar Orbit) to access every part of the world. The satellite is going to help in montoring live images of any location requested by the users. The satellite can also play a part of Natural Disaster and Geospatial Monitoring while simultaneously being the eyes of the public users from space.

To make it accessible to the public we decided to make an app . An app is one of the best ways to incorprate the general public The aim of the app is to make the public experience the opportunity to travel to space and enjoying the spectacular views by mimicking it using a satellite were they can take their own pictures from space ! Enjoy spectacular views and connect with others all around the world on a budget ! Hopefully this app will make people realize how fragile our home planet is .

What will we do to launch the satellite soon? In the future weeks Team delta Cosmos is going to find more sponsors. As we want our satellite to be as perfect as possible. Next we will finish the front-end and the back end of the satellite. Then we will start our fundraisers to buy all the necessary components of the satellite. In our final stage we will start building a real satellite.
