Delta Cosmos is a team of 9 members in SSERD ranging from different age groups starting from 13 to 18 attending from India and Egypt. We have designed a satellite model and a mobile application, for the public to get access to images taken by satellites. We all are looking forward to making this project live based on its use case, our efforts, and the benefits of educating young scientists further.
