  • Will the app run on both Android and IOS Operating Systems?

    Yes, the app is designed particularly to run on both the OS (Operating System) platforms and will include the same cool features through Android and IOS Operating systems.

  • Is the app free and is it international?

    Yes, the app is free for all users, and can be accessed by any user around the world

  • Where will the satellite be placed?

    The satellite is planned to have a specific Polar orbit in the LEO (Low Earth Orbit) region. With the advantages of Polar Orbit, we can access any part of the Earth in a number of orbits. Since, the satellite is a unique Cubesat, it is necessary to launch it into the Low Earth Region.

  • Is my personal data safe when I permit the app to access location?

    Yes, your personal data is safe, and is stored in a reliable ground-based server. We are not interested in selling your personal data to third-party users and cookies. We access the location for the only reason to provide you with the Reminders feature of the app and to access your time zone.

  • How is the satellite going to take pictures for several users?

    The satellite is going to take a single picture of a requested location and store it on the worldwide servers. The app makes the image accessible to many users who requested the same or nearby location at a time.
